PLH EXPERIENCE WORK

Quiz-its

Challenge Statement

How might we create a quick and fun activity for users to engage with to take a break from everyday stress.

User Research Interviews

Interview Participants Interview 7 users of various age range from 20 something millenials to 50-year-old professionals that enjoy playing social media quizzes or participating in trivia game participants.

Research Insights

Users like to test their general knowledge skills and enjoy learning new information through trivia questions. Users like to keep the results to themselves and seldom share their quiz results on social media. User reasons for taking a quiz or playing trivia vary from liking learning new fun facts to learning more about themselves through answering the questions. Users spend about 10 minutes a day /week engaged in a social or trivia quiz.
