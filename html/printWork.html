USA Today Network 2000-2017

I wore many hats during my tenure at USA Today Network newspapers as a visual journalist/newspaper designer. The titles I held included: Team Leader, Design Content Editor III, Art Director of All the Rage, and designer — all of which pertained to the planning, design, layout, and production of newspaper products within the company.

I worked on the presentation of newspapers in Tennessee, Florida, Mississippi and Virginia. Collaborated with various remote newspaper editors on the visual presentation of various daily front page news designs, enterprise projects, infographics, sports sections, features sections, and annual magazine projects. I also managed a team of designer that worked on the presentation of The Tennessean (Nashville, TN),The Clarion-Ledger (Hattiesburg, MS), The Hattiesburg American (Hattiesburg, MS) and The News Leader (Staunton, VA), and All The Rage (Nashville, TN).
